HAM Club Events and Activities

About

Our Ham Radio Club is a diverse community of radio enthusiasts who share a passion for amateur radio. Whether you're an experienced operator or just getting started, we offer a welcoming environment. Our activities include meetings to discuss the latest technology, training for new operators, field days, contests, community service, educational tours, and outings. We also provide a platform for buying, selling, or trading equipment. Beyond radio, we organize social gatherings to build friendships. Join us to explore the world of amateur radio, connect with like-minded individuals, and be part of a vibrant community dedicated to the art of radio communication. We look forward to having you on board!
